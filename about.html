About Medivo

Smarter Care. Better Outcomes.

Medivo is a health technology company building connected medical solutions that help healthcare organizations deliver better care through modern software, automation, data intelligence, and secure digital experiences.

Mission

To simplify healthcare delivery through connected technology.

Medivo exists to reduce the friction between patients, providers, data, workflows, and care services. The company is building a future where clinical systems are easier to use, more intelligent, and more connected.

What makes Medivo different

EMR is the anchor, but Medivo is being positioned as a health technology ecosystem with patient engagement, AI, automation, virtual care, billing, analytics, interoperability, and managed services around it.

Core Values

Human care, digital trust, intelligent execution.

C

Care

Technology should serve patients, physicians, and care teams.

T

Trust

Security, privacy, transparency, and auditability must be built into health technology.

I

Innovation

AI, automation, and interoperability should create practical healthcare value.

Leadership Direction

Built for long-term healthcare infrastructure.

Medivo is being shaped with an enterprise mindset: scalable architecture, strong product design, compliance awareness, and a roadmap that extends from EMR to broader digital health infrastructure.

Medivo is a health technology company building smarter digital care infrastructure, with EMR as the star platform and a broader suite of connected medical solutions.
